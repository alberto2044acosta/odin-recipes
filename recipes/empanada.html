Empanada

Description:

This is one of my favorites recipes, it is simple (but delicious), it is cheap (but with quality), it can be made in different ways (but all of them makes you happy), yes, if you don't now yet, I am talking about the empanada.

The origins of this recipe are in Asia, in Persia especifically, and then this dish became more and more popular along a lot of countries, mostly in latin america and some places in Spain. Now that we understand a little bit more of the empanada, let's figure out how the empanada is (a normal version):

         Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Butter or oil

         Steps:

  1. The first step is make the empanada dough with flour, water and salt
  2. The second step is to shape it into an empanada
  3. The third step is to add the chesse
  4. The fourth step is put it in the frying pan with oil (a hight quantity) and butter
