Mofongo

Description:

Today I will show you one of the best recipes of the Dominican Republic: Mofongo. This food is very popular along all the country, mostly in Moca (a Dominican city) where there are many stores to buy it. It has a very well taste, everyone should try it. Some people believe that this food was built mixing african, Spaniards and native ingredients on the age of colonization, that started in 1492. There are many ways Mofongo can be made, you can eat it with rice, fish, different kinds of meat, shrimp, etc. By the way, I will show you the most common way to cook it:

         Ingredients:

  • Green plantains
  • Cheese
  • Fried chicken
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt

         Steps:

  1. The first step is to put oil in the pot
  2. The second step is to mash the green plantains
  3. The third step is put them in the pot
  4. The fourth step is to grate the cheese on top
  5. The fifth step is to add garlic and salt
  6. The sixth step is to wait until it is well cooked
